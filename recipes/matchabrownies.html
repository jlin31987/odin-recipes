Matcha Brownies

Matcha brownies that are a perfect balance between sweet and earthy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons matcha green tea powder
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8x8-inch baking dish

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (177°C). Line an 8x8-inch baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. Combine the flour, sugar, and matcha powder in a large bowl.
  3. Beat melted butter, eggs, and vanilla with an electric mixer in a second bowl. Stir in the dry ingredients.
  4. Pour the batter into the baking dish. Use the back of a spoon or spatula to spread the batter evenly.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes until you can prick the center with a toothpick and it comes out clean. Remove from the oven, place on a cooling rack, and leave to cool completely in the pan.
  6. Cut into 9 squares and serve.
