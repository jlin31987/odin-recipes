Coffee Jelly

A dessert from the show called "The Disastrous Life of Saiki K."

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon gelatin powder
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 cups brewed black coffee
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 8x8-inched square baking dish

Steps:

  1. Mix gelatin powder with 1/4 cup of water in a small bowl.
  2. Heat the coffee and sugar together in a small saucepan over medium heat until it comes to a simmer.
  3. COol for ten minutes at room temperature before pouring the coffee jelly mixture into an 8x8-inched square baking dish. Refrigerate for at least four hours or until set.
  4. Once the cofee jelly sets, cut it into 1/2-inch cubes.
  5. Serve in stemware or indiviual bowls. Top with your favorite topping of choice between whipped cream, condensed milk, or vanilla ice cream.
