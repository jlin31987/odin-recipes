Honey Milk Tea

One of the most popular drinks in the world!

Ingredients:

  • 2 orange pekoe tea bags
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 5 ice cubes
  • 4 teaspoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 teaspoons honey

Steps:

  1. Steep the tea bags in boiling water until water color turns dark red, takes around 3 to 5 minutes. Discard tea bags afterwards and let the tea cool.
  2. Combine ice cubes, sweetened condensed milk, and honey in a glass. Pour tea and mix well.
  3. Enjoy your newly made honey milk tea!
